Hampi

Hampi 5 Day's 4 Night's

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Karnataka, is a mesmerizing blend of history and natural beauty. Known for its ancient temples, stunning boulder-strewn landscapes, and rich Vijayanagara Empire heritage, Hampi offers an unforgettable journey back in time. Explore iconic sites like the Virupaksha Temple, Vittala Temple with its Stone Chariot, and the Lotus Mahal, each showcasing exquisite architecture. Wander through the Hampi Bazaar, trek up Matanga Hill for panoramic views, and relax by the serene Tungabhadra River. A trip to Hampi is a perfect escape for history buffs, adventure seekers, and nature lovers alike.

Andharbar Forest Trek

The Andharban Forest Trek, located near Tamhini Ghat in Maharashtra, is a stunning journey through dense, mist-laden forests, earning its name "Andharban," meaning "Dark Forest." This one-day trek spans approximately 13–15 km and takes you along lush green trails with breathtaking views of the Kundalika Valley, Bhira Dam, and numerous cascading waterfalls, especially vibrant during the monsoon season. The trek begins at Pimpri village and ends near Bhira Dam, offering a mix of adventure, natural beauty, and tranquility. Perfect for nature lovers and trekking enthusiasts, Andharban is a moderate-level trek best experienced during the monsoon or winter for its serene and picturesque charm.

Harihar Fort Trek

The Harihar Fort Trek in Maharashtra is an exhilarating adventure, known for its unique, nearly vertical stone-cut staircase that challenges even experienced trekkers. Located near Igatpuri, the fort is perched at an altitude of around 3,676 feet and offers panoramic views of lush green valleys, the Western Ghats, and surrounding forts like Brahmagiri and Anjaneri. The trek, which takes about 3–4 hours to ascend, is moderate to difficult, with its steep, narrow rock-cut steps being the highlight. At the summit, you can explore the remnants of the fort, a small temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, and a water cistern. Best visited during monsoon or winter, the Harihar Fort Trek is a must for thrill-seekers and history enthusiasts.
